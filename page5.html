While little legal recourse could be found, people took matters into their own hands. Like a modern day Boston Tea Party, people on masse took their Cyber headsets and had either thrown them away or destroyed them. Many photos from the time show that mass burnings were a popular method of disposal.

While there is not currently a way to reverse Brain Corruption, several medical breakthroughs funded by Cyber were able to slow down or even in some cases stop the progression of corruption. Additionally, Cyber, of their own volition, generously gave rebates to all their surviving customers with the expressed purpose of aiding the people affected with their medical bills. The generation that would emerge from this era would be known as the "Corrupted Generation".

It would appear that this is the end of one of the most significant events in our technological world. But like a phoenix, Cyber would not be gone for good...
