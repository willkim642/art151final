In the year 2032, The social media platform Cyber releases a technological revolution. The CyberVerse. It becomes an instant sensation. By putting on a specialized headset, the person's mind is temporarily moved out of the physical body and then uploaded to the CyberVerse.

Within hours it would capture the cultural zeitgeist. At it's peak, approximately half of the entire human population were active users. It becomes more than just a social media platform.

However after three years, the course of the CyberVerse would be changed forever.
